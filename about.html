Our Story

Health Meets Indulgence

The Maida-Free Mission

Founded in Bhairahawa, Velvet Bakes was born out of a simple goal: to make celebrations healthier without losing the magic of a traditional patisserie.

We replace refined flour (Maida) with stone-ground whole wheat (Atta) and use organic jaggery instead of white sugar. The result? A nutrient-dense bite that tastes like pure luxury.

100% Whole Grain

Zero Maida. Only stone-ground whole wheat for natural fiber.

Natural Sweetness

Organic jaggery and honey over refined white sugar.

Local Sourcing

Fresh ingredients from the farms of Rupandehi.
